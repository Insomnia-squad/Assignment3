Group Processes

Due to the online nature of the group, everyone had to deal with online communication rather than face to face or getting everyone in one room at the same time. Reflecting on assignment 2, we were able to begin on time and not waste any time during the earlier stages of the assessment. This demonstrated good communication between the group members which is shown to persist through the regular use of Microsoft Teams. We were able to distribute the work without any issues and build on top of each other's ideas. We were also able to effectively split the responsibilities when using GitHub and all contribute to the repository. We felt comfortable depending on each other when needed and often communicated even outside of scheduled meetings as a group. However, we did more work when approaching towards the end of the assignment, and failed to put up any drafts or update along the way until the assignment which decreased our consistency. In Terms of introducing changes for this assignment, we planned to have shorter valuable meetings. Then, try and achieve the goals set for every meeting and finally, aim for a more spread workload as opposed to doing all near the due date of the assignment.
