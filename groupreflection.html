Steven

As a group, we functioned extremely well together – excelling in communication especially. We very often exchanged and built on top of each other’s thoughts and ideas which lead to a reasonably pleasant website. Unlike during the previous assignment, we became more familiar and open with each other which boosted our efficiency. This is made apparent through our shorter and more concise team meetings. Previously, we would sometimes reach almost an hour and a half. However, throughout this assignment we have consistently reached meetings that last for 20-30 minutes. We would also regularly communicate with each other outside of Microsoft Teams through an Instagram group chat whenever there was something important that needed to be discussed. In hindsight, the use of the Instagram group chat was a very effective means of communication. Most of us were already accustomed to the app and the push notifications seemed to be a lot more consistent when trying to get the attention of fellow group members compared to Microsoft Teams.
