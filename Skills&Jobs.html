Detailed Description

Skills & Jobs

Let us suppose that a group of venture capitalists hears about your project, and is so impressed that they wish to fund six months of further development. You will be the manager of a team of four to six people to deliver the project outcomes. What position descriptions would now be appropriate? Write position descriptions for the people you would employ to take your project to the next phase. You need to consider the appropriate skills. This may include specific technical expertise, teamwork experience, leadership and management skills, and innovative thinking.

UX/UI Designer

Overview: As Fade’s UX designer, you will be responsible for gathering in-depth insights into the user’s psychology and providing intuitive application design. We aim to create an application where everyday consumers can control their inventory and alleviate the amount of food getting spoiled. So, we’re looking for a talented UX designer with an eye for detail to enhance this product and take it to the next level. Ultimately, you will make our product more user-friendly and intuitive to attract and retain customers.

As a UX designer, you will:

  • BSc in Design, Computer Science, Engineering or a related field OR 2+ years' experience in the design field.
  • Extensive knowledge of wireframe tools (e.g. Wireframe.cc and InVision, etc)
  • Up-to-date knowledge of design software like Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop
  • Good time-management skills
  • Strong communication skills to collaborate with various stakeholders
  • Knowledge of Usability Factors in Human-Computer Interaction is a plus

You'd have:

  • Develop UI mock-ups and prototypes that clearly illustrate how the application functions and create original graphic designs (e.g. images, sketches, etc)
  • Conduct layout adjustments based on user feedback
  • Communicate design ideas and prototypes to developers
  • Identify creative ways to troubleshoot UX problems (e.g. usability, findability)
  • Stay aware of the competitor products and industry trends

Application Developer

Overview: Your goal as an Application developer at Fade is to bring our application design to life. Essentially, we are building an application for consumers who would like to keep track of their food inventory making individuals and families aware of the food they are intaking. Thereby, avoiding food waste. If you’re passionate about translating code into a user-friendly app, that will eventually make a difference in an individual’s life, we would be glad to have you in our team.

You will:

  • Support the entire application lifecycle (concept, design, test, release and support)
  • Evaluating the application and performing updates and modifications
  • Liaise with the Product development team to plan new features

Skills required:

  • Degree in Computer Science or related field
  • Working knowledge of programming languages such as Java/JavaScript and Python
  • Solid background in handling databases
  • Knowledge of software design and programming principles
  • Proficient in Mathematics and analytical thinking
  • Strong communication and team-working skills

Product Manager:

Overview: You are responsible for identifying market opportunities, shaping the product being built, and determining the return on investment. You will be expected to drive change and move forward through a vision throughout the team. This means working at all levels of managing the team and gathering all the data from user testing and convert it into an implementable strategy.

You will:

  • Ensure product areas are an activity in line with our high expectations and be able to determine, diagnose, and advocate concepts to boost our performance
  • Use your robust technical background to debate and orient complicated issues
  • LSet the direction of your team; anticipate strategic challenges via thoughtful coming up with, each from personnel and technical point of view
  • Foster a cooperative, ambitious, and outcome-driven culture
  • Work for hands-on with the team to style new solutions and war technical issues yourself

Ideally you'd have:

  • 3+ years of industry experience in a similar role
  • A detail-oriented and analytical mindset, with emphasis on accuracy
  • Leadership skills; Capable of coaching, planning a team
  • Reasonable coding ability, familiar with technologies such as python and java

Nice to have:

  • Prior start-up experience to help us grow responsibly
  • Experience managing teams up to 10-30 people
